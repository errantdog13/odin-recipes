Pancakes

Photo Credit: Allrecipes Magazine

Description

This is a simple and delicious pancake recipe that's perfect for a quick breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients into a mixing bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the wet ingredients.
  3. Gradually combine the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients, stirring until well combined and smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately ¼ cup for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form and edges dry (about 1 or 2 minutes). Flip and cook until golden brown on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
  5. Serve hot with your favorite toppings.
