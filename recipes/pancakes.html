Pancakes

This and all other recipes on this site are from All Recipes unless otherwise noted.

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
10 mins 10 mins 20 mins

Serves 4

Easy Pancake Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg

Directions

Step 1

Combine dry ingredients into a mixing bowl.

Step 2

In a separate bowl, whisk together the wet ingredients.

Step 3

Gradually combine the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients, stirring until well combined and smooth.

Step 4

Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately ¼ cup for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form and edges dry (about 1 or 2 minutes). Flip and cook until golden brown on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.

Step 5

Serve hot with your favorite toppings.
