Low-Carb Zucchini Pasta

Please see original recipes at Allrecipes Magazine unless otherwise noted.

Photo Credit: Allrecipes Magazine

Description

Low-carb zucchini pasta is a healthy alternative to traditional pasta. It’s made with zucchini noodles, which are low in carbs and calories, making it a great option for those following a low-carb or keto diet. The zucchini noodles are typically sautéed with olive oil and seasoned with salt and pepper, creating a delicious and satisfying dish that can be enjoyed on its own or with your favorite sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 zucchini, peeled
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup water
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Use a vegetable peeler to cut long, wide strips down one side of one zucchini until you reach the seeds. Turn zucchini and repeat on three remaining sides. Repeat with remaining zucchini. Discard zucchini cores.
  2. Stack zucchini strips on top of each other, then cut thin strips lengthwise along the stack; strips will resemble spaghetti noodles.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add zucchini and water; cook and stir until zucchini is tender but still crisp, about 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
